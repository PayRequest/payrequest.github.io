Cookies

To enrich and perfect your online experience, PayRequest.io uses "Cookies", similar technologies and services provided by others to display personalized content, appropriate advertising and store your preferences on your computer.

A cookie is a string of information that a website stores on a visitor's computer, and that the visitor's browser provides to the website each time the visitor returns. PayRequest.io uses cookies to help PayRequest.io identify and track visitors, their usage of https://payrequest.io, and their website access preferences. By continuing to navigate our website without changing your cookie settings, you hereby acknowledge and agree to PayRequest.io's use of cookies.
