Frequently asked questions

PayRequest is a Payment Platform where you can create payment links via Stripe or Mollie, and directly send reminder to your customers via clients via email or sms. With PayRequest you can create Beautiful Payment Links and change the look & feel of your payment link pages, add your own logo, change the company avatar or add even your own background.
A payment link is a unique link you can share via email or sms with contains a custom amount and a personal message for the reciever, on the payment link page they can choose a payment method like credit card an pay the requested amounnt, after a succesfull payment you will get a notification about it.
Signup for Payrequest and creating payment links is free, you only pay the transactions costs of Mollie or Stripe, and we topup that with 2% service fee to cover the costs of our platform.
For now we only support and Mollie & Stripe because this are big name in the PSP market, we are planning to support more PSP in the future, also CoinBase & PayPal is on our roadmap, if you need any other PSP please contact us and we will add it to our roadmap.
